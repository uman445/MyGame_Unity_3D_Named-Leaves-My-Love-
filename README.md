# MyGame_Unity_3D_Named-Leaves-My-Love-
Developed with Unity3D engine, version 2022.2 LTS.

Why do I want to develop this game?
    Recently, AI technology is developing rapidly. More and more people have terrible feelings such as anxiety and insecurity, and even doubt that humans have created a monster to destroy and negate human beings.
    But in my eyes, I appreciate the situation that drives human beings to resist AI, because it proves that we are alive and we are human. We can adapt ourselves to the environment and change the environment. It is precisely this point, not anything else, that ensures human survival forever.

    In fact, I also want to make money and improve my game development skills.
